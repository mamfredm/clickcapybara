Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At Click-Capybara, we care about your privacy and want to keep things simple and transparent. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have as a visitor from the EU.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us operate and improve our website:

  • Personal Data: Only if you contact us directly (e.g., your email address).
  • Usage Data: Anonymous or pseudonymous analytics data, device information, pages viewed, and general technical logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the website.
  • Respond to messages you send us.
  • Understand how visitors use our site (analytics).
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies only with your consent. Our cookie banner (powered by Silktide) allows you to manage your preferences at any time.

Google Analytics & Google Ads (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand visitor behavior. Data may be processed on servers in the USA. Tracking only starts after you give consent through the cookie banner.

Microsoft Advertising

We may use Microsoft Advertising to measure campaign performance. Tracking is only active if you consent to advertising cookies.

Hosting: GitHub Pages

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc.). GitHub processes technical logs such as IP addresses to deliver the website securely. Servers may be located in the United States.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or trade personal information. Data is shared only with trusted service providers (GitHub, Google, Microsoft) as required to operate this website.

5. Your Data Rights

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time (via the cookie manager).
