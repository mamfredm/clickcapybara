Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At Click-Capybara, we care about your privacy and want to keep things simple and transparent. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have as a visitor from the EU.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us operate and improve our website:

  • Personal Data: Only if you contact us directly (e.g., your email address).
  • Usage Data: Anonymous or pseudonymous analytics data, device information, pages viewed, and general technical logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the website.
  • Communicate with you when you contact us.
  • Understand how visitors use the site (analytics).
  • Comply with applicable EU laws.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies only with your consent. Our cookie banner (powered by Silktide) allows you to choose whether analytics or advertising cookies may be set.

Google Analytics & Google Ads (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 may use cookies or similar technologies. Data may be transferred to servers in the United States. All tracking only activates after your consent.

Microsoft Advertising

We may use Microsoft Advertising to measure campaign performance. Data is processed according to your cookie settings.

Hosting: GitHub Pages

Our website is hosted by GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc.), part of Microsoft. When you visit the site, GitHub automatically processes technical logs (e.g., IP address, browser type) to deliver the website securely. GitHub servers may operate in the United States.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or trade personal information. Data is only shared with trusted service providers (GitHub, Google, Microsoft) as needed to run the site.

5. Your Data Rights (EU/DSGVO)

If you live in the EU or EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Access your stored personal data.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
